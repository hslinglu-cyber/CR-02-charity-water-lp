The Crisis

703 million people still have no clean water.

Women and girls walk an average of 6km every day just to collect water — water that makes their families sick. Children miss school. Mothers miss work. Entire communities are trapped in a cycle that clean water can break.

When you support charity: water, you're funding transparent, traceable water projects. You can see on a map exactly where your money went and who it helped.
